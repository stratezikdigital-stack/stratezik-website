# Organic Social That Sounds Like You, Not a Template

Stratezik runs organic social media for Toronto, Scarborough, and GTA businesses primarily on LinkedIn and Facebook, the two platforms where most of our clients' customers actually pay attention. The goal is a steady, on-voice presence that builds real audience and supports the rest of your marketing, not a feed full of stock images and motivational quotes.

## What you get

- **A monthly content calendar** built around your business, not a generic template.
- **Post copy in your voice**, drafted by an operator and reviewed before it goes out.
- **Consistent scheduling** across the platforms that matter for your buyers.
- **Community management** during business hours, including comments and DMs.
- **Monthly reporting** on reach, engagement, and follower growth.

## How we run it

Every engagement begins with a short platform audit so we are not starting from theory. Then we plan a month of content at a time around your real business, your offers, and the questions your customers actually ask. Posts go through copy review before publishing, then ship on schedule.

Where it makes sense, we coordinate with [SEO and AEO](/services/seo-aeo) so your social content reinforces what you are already publishing on the site, instead of pulling in a different direction.

## Proof

This work is run by the same team that built Stratezik's own founder-led content and the AI agent system that helps produce it. The voice you see in our material, plainspoken, opinionated, operator-first, is the same approach we bring to client social.

**Do you produce video?** Not in-house. We can write video briefs and short-form scripts, then refer to a creative partner for production.

**Can you handle paid social too?** Yes, through our [Paid Social Ads](/services/paid-social) service, and we coordinate when both are active.
