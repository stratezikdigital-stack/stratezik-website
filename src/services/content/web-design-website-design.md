# Full Websites Built to Convert and Last

A small business website is a permanent marketing asset and a daily sales tool. Stratezik designs and builds full websites for Toronto and GTA businesses in Webflow, Framer, or custom code, with SEO and AODA compliance baked in from day one rather than bolted on later.

## What you get

- **Discovery and site strategy** before any design happens.
- **Wireframes and UX structure** that earn their layout.
- **Operator-led copywriting** through our content team.
- **Visual design** following your brand pack, or Stratezik direction if you do not have one.
- **A CMS your team can actually use** after handoff.
- **On-page SEO basics** built in: meta, schema, semantic HTML, sitemap.
- **AODA / WCAG 2.0 AA compliance** as standard.
- **Launch support and a 30-day window** to fix anything we missed.

## How long it takes

A typical website project runs six to ten weeks from kickoff to launch. Discovery and strategy first, then wireframes and copywriting in parallel, then design, then build and QA across devices and browsers, then launch and warranty.

## Common questions

**Webflow or Framer or custom?** Most Toronto small businesses are best served by Webflow or Framer for ease of editing after handoff. Custom is for teams with specific technical requirements.

**Do you do e-commerce?** Shopify setups as a project add-on. Full custom e-commerce platforms are outside our scope.

**Is AODA compliance really required?** For Ontario businesses, yes. We build to WCAG 2.0 AA as standard, not as an upsell.
