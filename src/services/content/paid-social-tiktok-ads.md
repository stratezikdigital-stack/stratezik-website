## Common questions

**Is TikTok worth it for B2B?** Almost never. There are exceptions for founder-led personal brand and niche professional services, but for most B2B work the audience is not there and LinkedIn or Meta is better.

**Do I need to be on camera myself?** It helps for founder-led brands, but TikTok ads can also use user-generated-content style edits, product demos, and creator partnerships.

**How big does my budget need to be?** Smaller than people assume, because creative matters more than spend. We size it before we start.
