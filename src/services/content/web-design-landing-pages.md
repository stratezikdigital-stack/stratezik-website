# Landing Pages That Convert From the First Click

If you are spending on paid ads, the page they land on is doing half the work, and most pages do not. Stratezik designs and builds single, conversion-focused landing pages for Toronto and GTA businesses running paid campaigns or specific launches. Built fast, built right, ready to test.

## What you get

- **A focused page** with one job, one CTA, no distractions.
- **Strategy and wireframe** before design, so the structure earns its place.
- **Copy by an operator**, written for the searcher who clicked.
- **Webflow, Framer, or hand-coded** depending on what fits your stack.
- **Conversion tracking** wired in before launch, not after.
- **AODA / WCAG 2.0 AA compliance** as standard.
- **30-day post-launch support** to fix anything we missed.

## How it works

A landing page engagement runs about two to three weeks from kickoff to live. We start with strategy and wireframe, then copywriting, then visual design, then build, then QA across devices and browsers. Conversion tracking and AODA compliance are baked in from the start, not added at the end.

## Common questions

**How long does a landing page take?** Typically two to three weeks from brief to launch.

**Webflow or Framer?** Both work. We pick based on your team's ability to edit the page after we hand off and the specific design needs.

**Will the page be on my domain?** Yes, on a subdomain or a clean URL path, set up so search and analytics see it as part of your site.
