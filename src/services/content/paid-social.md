## Common questions

**Should I run Meta or LinkedIn?** Meta for broad consumer demand and most local services. LinkedIn for B2B and higher-deal-size services where the click cost is justified by the eventual contract.

**How important is creative?** On paid social, creative is most of the campaign. We provide creative direction and briefs and refer to a creative partner for production when needed.

**Can you handle TikTok and Pinterest?** Yes, where the audience is genuinely there. We do not push a channel that does not fit just to spend budget.
