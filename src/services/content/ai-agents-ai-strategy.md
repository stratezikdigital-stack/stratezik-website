# AI Strategy for Founders Who Want a Real Roadmap

Most AI initiatives fail because nobody decided what to automate before they started building. Stratezik runs AI strategy engagements that answer that question first: which parts of your business will pay back from agents, in what order, and what the org chart looks like once they are in place.

## What you get

- **An agent org-chart design** that maps roles, handoffs, and autonomy levels.
- **Context engineering** that turns your operating knowledge into an asset agents can use.
- **A prioritised roadmap** for what to build first, second, and never.
- **An honest read** on what is automatable today and what is not yet ready.
- **The reference build** to look at while you decide, because we run on our own system.

## How we run it

A strategy engagement typically runs two to four weeks. We start with a discovery of your operations, then design the agent org that would fit your business, then write the context engineering that the agents will need, then deliver a deployment roadmap with effort estimates per phase.

The output is a real plan you can hand to a build team, whether that team is us under [Agent Development](/services/ai-agents/agent-development), your internal engineers, or another firm. Sits under our [AI Agents parent service](/services/ai-agents).

## Proof

The same agent architecture we recommend is the one we use to run Stratezik. You can see the reference build before you commit, which is unusual for AI consulting and is the strongest proof we know how to do this.

## Common questions

**Will I get a real plan or a slide deck?** A real plan. Org chart, context files, deployment phases, what each agent does and what it does not. Not a vision document.

**Do I need this if I just want to build one agent?** Not always. For a single, scoped agent, skip to [Agent Development](/services/ai-agents/agent-development). Strategy matters when you are committing to a system.

**How long does the strategy engagement take?** Typically two to four weeks from discovery to delivered roadmap.
