## Common questions

**Should I run Google Ads or Microsoft Ads?** Most businesses should start with Google, then add Microsoft when the Google account is mature. Microsoft often has cheaper clicks but lower volume in Canadian markets.

**How long until I see results?** Search ads start producing data immediately. Real optimisation gains usually appear by the end of month two, with stronger compounding from month three on.

**What is the minimum budget?** Email us and we will tell you, honestly, whether Google Ads makes sense at your spend level. If it does not, we will say so.
