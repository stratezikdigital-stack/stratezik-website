## Common questions

**When is LinkedIn worth it?** When your deal size is high enough that an $8 to $15 click is cheap relative to a single closed deal. Usually B2B services, enterprise sales, recruiting, and high-margin professional services.

**LinkedIn or Meta for B2B?** Meta can reach business buyers cheaper, but LinkedIn reaches them more precisely. Many B2B clients run both, with LinkedIn carrying the targeting and Meta carrying the volume.

**Do you handle creative?** Direction and briefs. Production through creative partners, because LinkedIn rewards a tone most generic agency creative does not nail.
