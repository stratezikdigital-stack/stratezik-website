## Common questions

**Do I need a landing page or a full site?** Start with a landing page if you have a specific campaign or launch. Build the full site when you need a permanent home for the business, with multiple services and ongoing content.

**Webflow, Framer, or custom?** Most Toronto SMBs are best served by Webflow or Framer for ease of ongoing edits. Custom is for teams with specific technical needs.

**Is AODA compliance really required?** For Ontario businesses, yes. We build to WCAG 2.0 AA as standard, not as an upsell.
